Our Educational Programs

We offer comprehensive programs designed to nurture young minds from early childhood through primary education.

Early Childhood Development

Our ECD program focuses on holistic development through play-based learning, social interaction, and foundational skills.

  • Play Group (2-3 years)
  • Pre-Primary 1 (3-4 years)
  • Pre-Primary 2 (4-5 years)

Lower Primary

Grades 1-3 where we build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills through engaging activities.

  • Grade 1 (6-7 years)
  • Grade 2 (7-8 years)
  • Grade 3 (8-9 years)

Upper Primary

Grades 4-8 preparing students for secondary education with a rigorous curriculum and extracurricular activities.

  • Grade 4-6 (9-12 years)
  • Grade 7-8 (12-14 years)
  • KCPE Preparation

About Bright Academy

Founded in 2010, Bright Academy has grown from a small ECD center to a full-fledged primary school offering quality education from Early Childhood Development through Standard 8.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and character development. We believe every child has unique potential waiting to be unlocked.

With modern facilities, a low student-teacher ratio, and a curriculum that balances academics with co-curricular activities, we prepare students not just for exams, but for life.

Why Choose Bright Academy?

Balanced Curriculum

We combine the 8-4-4 curriculum with modern teaching methods and co-curricular activities for holistic development.

Qualified Teachers

Our teachers are TSC certified with specialized training in child-centered learning approaches.

Technology Integration

We incorporate digital literacy from an early age with our computer lab and smart classroom technologies.

Nutrition Program

Healthy, balanced meals prepared on-site by our nutritionist to support learning and growth.

Transportation

Safe, reliable school buses with trained attendants serving major neighborhoods.

Pastoral Care

We prioritize emotional well-being with counseling services and character development programs.
